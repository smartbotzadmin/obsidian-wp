## 🛠️ **How to Use the Page Builder**

### **Step 1: Access the Builder**
1. **Login to WordPress** as an administrator or editor
2. **Visit your website** (front-end, not admin)
3. **Look for the blue circular button** on the left side of your screen
4. **Click the button** to open the page builder panel

### **Step 2: Using Elements**
1. **Browse the Elements tab** to see available components
2. **Drag any element** from the panel to the drop zone on your page
3. **Click on text** to edit it directly (contenteditable)
4. **Use element controls** (edit/delete buttons) that appear on hover

### **Step 3: Using Templates**
1. **Switch to the Templates tab** in the builder panel
2. **Click on any template** to instantly add it to your page
3. **Customize the content** by clicking on text and images
4. **Mix and match** different templates to create unique layouts

### **Step 4: Page Settings**
1. **Go to the Settings tab** for global page options
2. **Set page title** and background colors
3. **Add custom CSS** for advanced styling
4. **Save your settings** when done

### **Step 5: Save Your Work**
1. **Click "Save Page"** to store your design
2. **Use "Preview"** to see your page without the builder interface
3. **"Clear All"** removes all content (use with caution)
